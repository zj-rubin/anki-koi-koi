Instant Win Deals · 即勝
Teshi · 手四6 pts
Four of a Suit in Hand
Being dealt all four cards of the same month/suit in your opening hand. The round ends immediately. If dealt to the field, the hand is void and redealt.
Kuttsuki · くっつき6 pts
Four Pairs in Hand
Being dealt four pairs of cards (two cards from four different months) in your opening hand. Round ends immediately.
Scoring Rules · 得点ルール
7+ Point Bonus×2
If your yaku total 7 or more points when the round ends, your score is doubled.
Koi-Koi Penalty×2
If your opponent called Koi-Koi and you subsequently form a yaku, your score is doubled (and their Koi-Koi earns them nothing).
Double Bonus×4
If you have 7+ pts AND your opponent called Koi-Koi, both multipliers apply for a total of ×4.
Oya-Ken · 親権1 pt
Dealer's Privilege
If neither player forms a yaku and cards run out, the dealer (Oya ★) gains 1 point and keeps the deal for the next round.
